❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are your booking policies?

We offer flexible booking with cancellation up to 2 hours before departure for a full refund. Choose from multiple payment methods for convenience.

How do I track my bus?

Visit our interactive map page to see real-time bus locations. You can track your journey and see estimated arrival times.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets, UPI, net banking, and buy-now-pay-later options. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 2 hours before departure through your account. Changes may incur additional fees.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The refund amount depends on when you cancel according to our cancellation policy.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and comply with all data protection regulations. Your information is never shared with third parties.
